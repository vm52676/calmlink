CalmLink · Student Wellbeing Micro‑App

A gentle digital space
for when everything feels a bit too loud.

CalmLink is a tiny, cozy tool for students to pause, breathe, and check in with themselves— no pressure, no judgement, just a soft corner of the internet that feels safe.

Grounding exercises
Mood check‑ins
Comfort messages
Safe contact reminders

Note: CalmLink is a wellbeing companion, not a medical tool. It's designed to sit alongside real‑life support from trusted adults, friends, and professionals.
